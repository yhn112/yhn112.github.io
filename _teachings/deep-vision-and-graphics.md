## Overview

Lecturer in the [Deep Vision and Graphics](https://github.com/yandexdataschool/deep_vision_and_graphics) course at YSDA (co-developed with Skoltech). Topics span ConvNet architectures, vision transformers, object detection, semantic segmentation, generative models (VAE, GAN, diffusion), and neural rendering (NeRF).
