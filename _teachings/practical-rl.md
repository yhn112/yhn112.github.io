## Overview

Mentored students in the Practical Reinforcement Learning course, a joint offering by HSE University and Yandex available on [Coursera](https://www.coursera.org/learn/practical-rl). Covered policy gradient methods, Q-learning, actor-critic architectures, and practical applications.
